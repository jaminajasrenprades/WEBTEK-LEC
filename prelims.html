Introduction to Internet and Web Fundamentals

This course introduces the Internet, World Wide Web (WWW), World Wide Web Consortium (W3C), standard markup language and services of the internet.

Internet

It is a network of networks that consists of millions of private, public, academic, business, and government networks, of local to global scope, that are linked by a broad array of electronuc, wireless and optical networking technologies.
Vinton Cerf is the Father of the Internet.

World Wide Web

It is a global information medium where users can read and write via computers connected to the Internet.
Tim-Berners Lee founded the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology with the support from the Defense Advanced Research Projects Agency (DARPA) and the European Commission.

The World of Mass Media and Communication without the World Wide Web

Without the web, the world would feel mundane, as we would still live in the old times. Information would only be found in newspapers, books, and in television, as well as the environment, though not as vast as opposed to the world of information with the web.
